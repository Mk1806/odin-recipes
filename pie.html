Banana Cream Pie Recipe

Description

A pie is a baked dish which is usually made of a pastry dough casing that contains a filling of various savoury ingredients.

Pies may be filled with fruit, nuts, brown sugar, sweetened vegetables or with thicker fillings based on eggs and dairy.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups milk
  • 3 egg yolks beaten
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1(9 inch) baked pastry shell, cooled
  • 4 bananas, sliced

    • Steps

  • Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 derees C).
  • Combine sugar, flour and salt in a saucepan.
  • Gradually stir in milk
  • Cook, stirring constantly, over medium heat until mixture boils and thickens.
  • Continue to stir and cook for 2 more minutes, then remove from the burner.
  • PLace egg yolk mixture to remaining hot mixture.
  • Return the saucepan to the burner and cook, stirring for 2 more minutes.
  • Remove the saucepan from the stove.
  • Stir in banana and vanilla.
  • Fill pastry shell with sliced banana.
  • Cover the pudding.
  • Bake untill filling sets, 12 to 25 minutes.
  • Chill pie for 1 hour before serving.
