Strawberry Cake Roll Recipe

Description

A roll cake, cream roll, roulade or Swiss log is a type of rolled sponge cake filled with whipped cream, jam, or icing.

The origins of the term are unclear; in spite of the name "Swiss roll".

The cake is believed to have originated elsewhere in Central Europe, possibly Austria or Slovenia.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs divided
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla sugar
  • 3 tablespoons confectioners's sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 2 tespoons white sugar
  • 3/4 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1/2 cup whipping cream
  • teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups finely chopped strawberries
  • 5 fresh strawberries, halved

    • Steps

  • Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 C).
  • LIne a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  • Combine egg yolks, 1/2 cup of sugar and 2 teaspoons vanilla extract.
  • Beat with an electric mixer until foamy.
  • Beat egg whites in a glass bowl until foamy.
  • Gradually add confectioners' sugar and continue beating until stiff peaks form.
  • Slide over the egg yolk mixture.
  • Sift flour and baking powder over the stiff egg whites.
  • Carefully fold everything into the egg yolk mixture until the batter in well combined.
  • Spread batter into the prepared baking sheet.
  • Bake in the preheated oven until sponge cake is set and lightly browned, 12 to 16 minutes.
  • Using a dish towel remove the parchment paper and roll the cake. Let it cool, an hour.
  • Beat 3/4 cups cream in a chilled glass with an electric mixer until frothy.
  • Add vanilla sugar gradually, continue to beat until stiff peaks form.
  • Fold in strawberies with a spatula.
  • Carefully unroll sponge cake.
  • Spread strawberry cream on top, leaving a litte room on the edges.
  • Allow to chill in the refrigerator for 1 hour.
  • Beat 1/2 cup cream in a chilled glass with an electric mixer until frothy.
  • Add vanilla sugar gradually, continuing to beat until stiff peaks form.
  • Spread all over the strawberry roll and garnish with strawberry halves.
